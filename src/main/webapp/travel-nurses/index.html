Travel Nurses

Genie Healthcare recruits nursing professionals to supplement the staffing needs of medical facilities nationwide. Genie places registered nurses (RNs), licensed practical nurses (LPNs), licensed vocational nurses (LVNs), and certified nursing assistants (CNAs) in a variety of facility shifts, including per diem, contract, temp-to-perm, and direct hire placement.

All of our candidates are personally interviewed, pre-screened, and tested, and we believe they are the best in the business.
